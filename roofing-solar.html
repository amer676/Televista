The Problem

Door-Knocking Doesn't Scale Anymore

Your competitors are calling while you're still loading the truck. In roofing and solar, the first contractor on-site wins the job. Here's what's holding most contractors back:

01

Slow Speed-to-Lead

Storm damage creates a 72-hour window. Door-knocking one street at a time means you miss hundreds of homeowners who've already signed with someone else.

02

Canvassing Fatigue

Field reps burn out, get rejected, and cover limited ground. Tele-prospecting reaches 10× as many homeowners in the same time — without putting boots on every driveway.

03

Can't Scale for Storm Season

Hiring seasonal canvassers takes weeks. Televista can add callers to your campaign in days — so when a hail event hits, you're ready to flood the affected area with outreach immediately.
