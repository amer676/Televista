The Problem

DIY Cold Calling Doesn't Scale

Driving for dollars is exhausting. Skip tracing lists pile up. Your team is great at closing — not dialing. Here's what most real estate investors face:

01

Inconsistent Pipeline

Referrals dry up. Manual outreach is sporadic. One good month followed by nothing — you can't build a business on inconsistency.

02

No Time to Dial

You're running deals, negotiating contracts, and managing rehabs. Cold calling 200 numbers a day is a full-time job — one you shouldn't be doing yourself.

03

Untrained Virtual Assistants

Generic VAs don't know motivated seller conversations. They stumble on objections, misread tone, and burn through your best lists with nothing to show for it.

The Solution

Trained Callers Who Know Your Market

Every Televista caller is trained on distressed seller psychology, objection handling, and your specific ICP before they dial a single number. You get leads. We handle everything else.

Seller-Specific Training

Callers are briefed on distressed seller personas — pre-foreclosure, probate, vacant property, absentee landlords — and trained to handle each conversation naturally.

Target Zip Code Precision

You choose your counties and zip codes. Callers work your specific market — not a generic list. Every dial is focused on your acquisition criteria.

Warm Lead Handoff

When a seller shows interest, the lead is logged and passed to you immediately — with notes from the call so you can pick up exactly where the conversation left off.

Full Performance Reporting

Weekly call summaries, lead counts, and conversion data so you always know what's working and where to optimize your campaign.

Launch in 5 Days

Discovery call Monday. Caller trained by Wednesday. First dials going out by Friday. Most clients have appointments within 15 days of sign-up.

TCPA Compliant — Always

We honor the Do Not Call registry, call within legal hours, and use only human dialers. No robocalls, no automated blasting, no exposure for your business.
