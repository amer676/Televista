Televista vs Power ISA:
Dedicated, or Pooled and Part-Time?

Last updated: June 2026

Power ISA has been placing real estate inside sales agents since 2015 — an established name. But read the fine print and there are really two products: a part-time "dedicated" ISA, and a lower-cost "Speed to Lead" service that pools your leads with hundreds of other campaigns. We put Televista and Power ISA side by side — how dedicated the callers really are, what's included, and how transparent the pricing is. We're the premium, full-time, full-stack option. Here's the honest breakdown.

When to Choose Each

Power ISA is a legitimate, long-running ISA company. The question is which of their two models you'd actually be buying — and how much of the stack you have to bring yourself.

When to Choose Televista

You want full-time callers who are genuinely yours — not a part-time seat or a shared pool. Televista is for real estate investors, solar teams, roofers, insurance, and B2B companies who want a full-time dedicated team (25 callers, 10 on standby), the complete stack included (CallTools dialer, 100 local numbers, in-house list building and skip tracing, AI lead scoring, n8n and Zapier automation, CRM integration), transparent published pricing, weekly strategy calls, and a real-time dashboard. You don't supply the toolkit and you don't share your callers with hundreds of other campaigns. See our case studies for what a full-time dedicated operation produces. This is the premium option, built end to end.

When to Choose Power ISA

Fair credit: Power ISA has been doing real estate ISAs since 2015, with a large client base and structured coaching. If you're a real estate agent or team that already owns your CRM, data, and dialer and just wants a trained voice to plug into your existing follow-up — and you're comfortable with a part-time seat or a shared pay-per-lead model to keep costs down — they're an experienced choice. Just go in knowing which product you're buying: the affordable "Speed to Lead" tier is explicitly shared across many campaigns, the "dedicated" tier starts part-time, and a lot of the pricing only appears after a demo.

The Bottom Line

Power ISA is an established real estate ISA shop, and that experience is real. But there are two products behind the name: a part-time dedicated ISA, and a shared "Speed to Lead" pool that spreads your leads across hundreds of other campaigns — with much of the pricing held back until you book a demo, and the data, dialer, and CRM generally on you. Televista takes the opposite approach: full-time dedicated callers who work only your pipeline, the entire stack included, AI scoring and automation built in, transparent published pricing, and a real-time dashboard. If you already own your tools and want a low-cost part-time or pooled option, Power ISA fits. If you want a premium, full-time, fully-equipped team that's genuinely dedicated to you, that's Televista. See our full service breakdown or our guide to the best cold calling companies for more.
