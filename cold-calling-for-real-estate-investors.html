What We Do for Real Estate Investors

Our callers make 10,000+ outbound dials daily across distressed property, absentee owner, and off-market seller campaigns. Here's what you get when your pipeline runs on our phones.

Off-Market Seller Leads

Absentee owners, pre-foreclosure, probate, tax liens, code violations — we build and call the lists. Targeted to your markets and acquisition criteria so every dial has a purpose.

Qualified Appointments

Double qualification means every appointment on your calendar is a real opportunity. Motivation confirmed, timeline verified, property condition assessed, and price expectation set — before it ever hits your calendar.

Full List Building & Skip Tracing

County records, AI scoring, skip tracing — list building & skip tracing included, data billed at cost. We pull, scrub, and stack-rank your lists so callers hit the highest-probability sellers first.

Power Dialer + Local Numbers

CallTools with 100 rotating local numbers. Answer rates 3x higher than toll-free. Your caller shows up as a local number in every market you target — included in every plan.

CRM Integration

Leads pushed directly into GoHighLevel, HubSpot, REsimpli, Follow Up Boss, Podio, or Salesforce. Full call notes, motivation scores, and property details — nothing falls through the cracks.

Weekly Reports & Strategy Calls

You know exactly what's happening. Call volume, connect rate, appointments, QA scores — every week. You'll get a dedicated strategist reviewing performance with you, adjusting scripts, and flagging what's working before competitors catch on.

Who We Work With

Not a Generic List. Real Investor Types.

Cold calling for real estate investors isn't one-size-fits-all. Your strategy, your scripts, and your qualification criteria depend on how you invest. We build campaigns around that.

01

Wholesalers

Locking up off-market contracts and assigning them for profit. We find the motivated sellers — you close the deals and build your buyers list. See our wholesaler page →

02

Fix-and-Flip Investors

You need deal flow to keep your rehab crews busy and your capital working. We target distressed properties with equity upside — the kinds of deals that make the numbers work.

03

Buy-and-Hold Investors

Building a rental portfolio means finding below-market acquisitions consistently. We call absentee owners, tired landlords, and inherited property holders to source your next cash-flowing asset.

04

Land Investors

Acquiring vacant lots at pennies on the dollar. We target out-of-state landowners, tax-delinquent parcels, and heirs who don't know what they own — so you can make offers before anyone else.

05

Creative Finance Investors

Subject-to, seller financing, novations — these deals require a different conversation. Our callers are trained to identify sellers open to creative terms, not just cash offers.

06

Realtors

Prospecting for listing appointments with FSBOs, expired listings, and pre-foreclosures. We fill your calendar with homeowners ready to list — you focus on winning the appointment.

Cold Calling for Real Estate Investors FAQ

Common questions about our cold calling services for real estate investors.

A VA is a person you hire, train, manage, and hope shows up. Televista is a fully managed cold calling operation. We handle recruiting, training, scripting, list building, skip tracing, dialer technology, QA, and reporting. You don't manage anyone — you just receive qualified appointments on your calendar. When a VA quits, you start over. When a Televista caller needs replacing, we handle it in 48 hours with zero downtime.

No — you don't have to source or scrub anything yourself. We build your targeted lists in-house using county records, AI scoring, and professional skip tracing — absentee owners, pre-foreclosure, probate, tax liens, code violations, and more. Lists are built specifically for your target markets and acquisition criteria, with the raw data billed separately at cost as a transparent pass-through (never marked up). If you have your own data you'd like us to call through, we can do that too.

We can target virtually any property type or seller situation. Common campaigns include absentee owners, pre-foreclosure and notice of default, probate and inherited properties, tax lien and tax delinquent, code violations, vacant properties, high-equity homeowners, tired landlords, and expired listings. We build lists based on your specific acquisition criteria — property type, location, equity position, and owner situation.

Each dedicated caller typically generates 2-3 qualified appointments per day, which works out to 40-60 qualified appointments per month on the Starter plan. On the Growth plan with two callers, you're looking at 80-120 appointments per month. These are double-qualified — meaning the seller has confirmed motivation, timeline, property condition, and a realistic price expectation before the appointment lands on your calendar.

Yes. Cold calling is legal for real estate when done correctly. The key requirements are: calls must be made by live human callers (no robocalls or pre-recorded messages), numbers must be scrubbed against the National Do Not Call Registry, and callers must identify themselves and the purpose of the call. Televista handles all compliance — DNC scrubbing, call recording, caller ID compliance, and state-specific regulations. Every call we make is fully TCPA compliant.
