Televista vs Call Porter:
Full Comparison

Last updated: April 2026

This is the comparison people don't usually make — because these are fundamentally different services. Call Porter catches inbound calls from your marketing. Televista makes outbound calls to build your pipeline from scratch. One waits for the phone to ring. The other picks it up and dials. So which do you actually need? (Spoiler: maybe both. But if you can only pick one and you don't have a marketing machine feeding inbound leads already, we have thoughts. Strong ones. Pyramid-strong.)

When to Choose Each

This isn't really an either/or comparison — it's more like "which problem are you trying to solve right now?" Here's the honest breakdown.

When to Choose Televista

If you're sitting there waiting for your phone to ring and it's... not ringing... you need outbound. That's us. Televista is for businesses that want to proactively build pipeline instead of hoping their marketing generates enough inbound calls. We give you dedicated callers working only your campaign, a CallTools power dialer loaded with 100 local numbers, AI lead scoring that prioritizes the most likely-to-convert contacts, and full management with QA and weekly reporting. You don't set anything up. You don't manage anyone. You just get appointments. Works for real estate investors, solar companies, roofers, insurance agencies — really anyone who needs a steady flow of booked meetings. Check our services or the case studies for proof. We didn't survive 5,000 years of history by sitting around waiting for phone calls.

When to Choose Call Porter

Let's be real — Call Porter is good at what they do. If you're a real estate investor running direct mail, PPC, bandit signs, or any marketing that generates inbound seller calls, you need someone professional answering that phone. A motivated seller calling your number and getting voicemail? That's money walking away. Call Porter catches those calls, screens the leads, and books appointments using investor-focused scripts. They understand the RE investing world and they don't waste your time with tire-kickers. If your marketing is already generating inbound calls and you can't answer them all yourself, Call Porter is a solid choice. Just understand that their outbound calling is secondary — it's not their bread and butter.

The Bottom Line

Here's the thing nobody tells you: the smartest investors use both. Call Porter catches the inbound leads your marketing generates. Televista goes out and creates new leads through outbound cold calling. One is reactive. The other is proactive. Together, you're covered from both directions. But if you have to pick one? Ask yourself this: do you already have a marketing machine generating 20+ inbound seller calls a month? If yes, Call Porter makes sense. If no — or if you want to stop being dependent on ad spend and start building pipeline directly — Televista is the move. We're purpose-built for outbound at scale, with broader industry coverage and a lower entry price. Read our complete guide to cold calling companies to see how we compare across the board.
