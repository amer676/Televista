Which Delivers More Deals?

Two cold calling companies. Two very different business models. Lead Mining Pros made their name with skip tracing and pay-per-lead campaigns. Televista built a fully managed calling machine with flat pricing and zero surprises. So which one actually puts more deals on your desk? We broke it down based on real campaign data, pricing structures, and what clients actually experience. Fair warning: we're biased. But we're also right. (The pyramids weren't built by accident.)

When to Choose Each

Different companies, different strengths. Here's when each one makes the most sense — and yes, we'll actually give Lead Mining Pros credit where it's due.

When to Choose Televista

You want to hand someone the keys and say "get me appointments." That's it. That's the pitch. Televista is for people who don't want to think about skip tracing match rates, dialer setup, or whether their caller is also working three other campaigns at the same time. You get a dedicated caller who works only your account, a CallTools power dialer with 100 local numbers already loaded, AI lead scoring that prioritizes the best contacts first, and weekly reports so you always know what's happening. The pricing is flat — $1,250/month, no surprises, no "well that was a busy month so here's a bigger invoice." Works for real estate, solar, roofing, insurance, B2B — we're not a one-trick pony. Check out our full services or look at the actual numbers.

When to Choose Lead Mining Pros

Credit where it's due: Lead Mining Pros has solid skip tracing. If your main bottleneck is finding accurate phone numbers for your existing lists, they do that well. Their multi-channel approach — calls plus SMS follow-up — can also be effective if you want to hit leads from multiple angles simultaneously. The pay-per-lead model works for some people, especially if you're doing lower volume and want to pay only for what you get. Just know that the costs can add up fast during busy months, you'll likely need to handle more of the campaign management yourself, and the lead exclusivity question is worth asking about before you sign up. If you're comfortable being more hands-on with your campaigns, it can work.

The Bottom Line

Here's the honest truth: Lead Mining Pros is good at skip tracing and multi-channel outreach. But if you're looking for a fully managed cold calling operation where you don't have to worry about the moving parts — callers, data, dialer, QA, compliance, reporting — Televista was built for exactly that. Our flat pricing means no budget surprises. Our dedicated callers mean your campaign gets undivided attention. And our AI lead scoring means your callers are hitting the best numbers first, not just dialing randomly and hoping for the best. We didn't build the pyramids by hoping for the best.
