How It Works

Four Steps. Appointments on Your Calendar.

Most appointment setting companies hand you a list and wish you luck. We run the entire operation from first dial to booked meeting.

01

Discovery

We learn your business, your ideal client, your market, and your calendar availability. This is where we build the script and targeting strategy together.

02

Training

Your dedicated caller is trained on your offer, your objection patterns, and your qualification criteria. They don't just read a script — they understand your business.

03

Calling

Using a power dialer with 100+ rotating local numbers, your caller works through targeted lists daily. Every conversation is recorded and QA-reviewed.

04

Appointments Land

Qualified prospects are booked directly on your calendar with full notes from the conversation. You walk in prepared. No cold intros, no context-switching.
