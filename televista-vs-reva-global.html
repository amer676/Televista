Televista vs REVA Global:
Which Is Right for You?

Last updated: April 2026

Both Televista and REVA Global offer outbound calling for real estate businesses, but they're built for very different things. One is a fully-managed calling operation; the other is a VA staffing company. Here's how they actually compare — based on 200+ real campaigns, publicly verifiable pricing, and the features that actually move revenue. We'll be upfront about where we're the better fit and where we're not.

When to Choose Each

Neither option is universally better. The right choice depends on your business model, bandwidth, and what you actually need.

When to Choose Televista

If your idea of a good time is NOT micromanaging a VA's daily task list, you're in the right place. Televista is for people who want to hand off the entire calling operation — callers, dialer, data, QA, reporting — and just show up to close deals. We train the callers, we pull the lists, we run the power dialer with 100 local numbers, and we send you weekly reports so you know exactly what's happening. No setup on your end. No babysitting. Just appointments on your calendar. Works best for real estate investors, solar companies, roofers, and B2B teams. Check the actual numbers if you don't believe us.

When to Choose REVA Global

Look — REVA's not bad. They're a solid VA company. If you need someone to handle admin, data entry, customer service, and transaction coordination alongside some calling, REVA makes sense. Their VAs are versatile and can wear a lot of hats. It's also a decent choice if you're the type who likes to manage everything yourself — pick your own dialer, build your own lists, train your own people. Nothing wrong with that. Just know you're paying more and doing more. If cold calling is only 20% of what you need, REVA's generalist model might be the better fit.

The Bottom Line

REVA Global is a VA staffing company. Televista is a fully-managed calling operation. Two different models for two different buyers. If you want someone to handle cold calling end-to-end — callers, data, dialer, QA, and reporting — so you can focus on closing deals, Televista is purpose-built for that. If you need a generalist VA who can do calling alongside admin, data entry, and customer service, REVA gives you that flexibility. Pick the model that matches how you want to run your business. We're confident in ours — built on disciplined operations, measurable results, and systems that scale.
