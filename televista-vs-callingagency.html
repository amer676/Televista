Budget or Managed?

Let's break down what you're actually getting from each so you can decide what matters more: the cheapest price tag or the best ROI.

When to Choose Each

Budget and managed aren't inherently better or worse — they solve different problems. Here's the honest take on when each makes sense.

When to Choose Televista

You're tired of getting "leads" that are really just names of people who picked up the phone. You want appointments that actually close. Televista gives you a dedicated caller who knows your industry, your market, and your objection handles better than most of your in-house team. Every call gets reviewed by QA. Every lead gets AI-scored before it hits your CRM. You get weekly strategy calls where we look at real numbers — conversion rates, contact rates, appointment-to-close ratios — and adjust the campaign accordingly. It's built for real estate investors, solar, roofing, insurance, and B2B teams who measure success in closed deals, not dial counts. The case studies tell the whole story.

When to Choose CallingAgency

You need to test a new market fast and cheap. You've got a brand new territory, an untested offer, or a product launch where you just need to see if anyone bites — and you don't want to invest in a fully managed campaign until you know there's demand. CallingAgency's lower price point and high-volume capability makes it a reasonable tool for that specific use case. They can throw a lot of dials at a list quickly. Just know that you're getting a call center, not a calling partner. The callers rotate, the quality varies, and the management is on you. Think of it as a first date, not a relationship. If the numbers look promising, that's when you bring in a managed operation to actually convert at scale.

The Bottom Line

CallingAgency is a budget call center. Televista is a managed calling operation. One gives you dials. The other gives you deals. If you're optimizing for cost-per-dial and just need to spray a market with calls to see what sticks, CallingAgency will do the job at a lower price point. If you're optimizing for cost-per-closed-deal and want every appointment to actually be worth your time, Televista is built for that. We train the callers, manage the QA, score the leads, and meet with you weekly to make sure the campaign keeps getting better. You can keep buying the bulk frozen meals, or you can get the personal chef. Both feed you — only one makes it worth sitting down at the table.
