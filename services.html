What additional services does Televista offer?

Appointment Setting

Beyond lead generation, we book qualified meetings directly on your calendar. See how our dedicated appointment setting service delivers 2–3 meetings per caller per day.

Off-Market Lead Sourcing

We build and enrich your prospect lists from verified data sources — with a focus on off-market leads that match your ideal customer profile.

CRM Integration & Setup

We integrate directly into HubSpot and other CRMs. Leads are delivered straight to your system with full notes and call recordings.

Workflow Automation

Automate follow-ups, lead routing, and notifications. We set up the systems so nothing falls through the cracks.

Analytics & Reporting

Advanced data analytics using Python and MATLAB. Daily scorecards, weekly reports, and strategic insights to optimize performance.

Full Management

We don't just hand over callers—we manage the entire operation. QA, coaching, performance tracking, and continuous improvement.

We use advanced AI to filter and optimize your call lists, review call recordings, and deliver actionable performance insights. Our QA and technical team ensures every campaign is continually improved for results.

Our AI models analyze prospect data and call outcomes to score and prioritize leads, so your team focuses on the highest-value opportunities first.

AI Conversation Analysis

We use AI to analyze call transcripts for sentiment, objection handling, and conversion signals—delivering insights to improve scripts and agent performance.
