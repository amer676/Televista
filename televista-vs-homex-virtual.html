When to Choose Each

Different companies, different strengths. Here's who each one is actually built for.

When to Choose Televista

You already know cold calling works — you need the machine behind it to be smarter, faster, and more integrated. Televista is for real estate investors, solar companies, roofers, and B2B teams who want trained callers paired with real technology: AI lead scoring that prioritizes your hottest prospects, a CallTools power dialer punching through 100 local numbers, CRM integration with whatever platform you're running (GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, REsimpli, Podio — we connect to all of them), and n8n/Zapier automation that routes leads, triggers follow-ups, and keeps nothing falling through the cracks. We write scripts too — but we build dynamic conversation frameworks that evolve based on what's actually working. Not just "say this, then say that." Our callers think. Weekly strategy calls, real reporting dashboards, a 25-person team with 10 on standby. Check our case studies for the numbers.

When to Choose Homex Virtual Agency

If you're starting from zero — no scripts, no outbound process, no idea where to begin — Homex has a legitimate value proposition. They're process-first. They'll build your scripts using conversion psychology principles, structure your follow-up cadence, and train callers on a system designed to move prospects through a funnel. For companies that have never run outbound before and need someone to build the playbook from scratch, that structured approach can be genuinely helpful. Just know that their tech stack is lighter — no AI scoring, less automation, fewer CRM options. If you need the foundation built, Homex can do that. If you need the foundation plus the engine plus the rocket fuel... well, you know where we're going with this.

The Bottom Line

Homex Virtual Agency focuses on "conversion psychology." We focus on actually converting. There's a difference. Both companies write scripts and set appointments. But Televista wraps those calls in AI lead scoring, power dialing with 100 local numbers, deep CRM integration, and automated follow-up sequences that ensure no lead goes cold. Homex builds solid outbound processes — we won't deny that. But process without technology is like having a great recipe and cooking over a campfire. Televista gives you the recipe AND the commercial kitchen. Transparent pricing from $1,250/mo, no long-term contracts, live in 5 business days.
