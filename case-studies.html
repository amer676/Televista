Real Estate Wholesaling

Tampa, FL — From 0 to 90+ Leads/Month

733% ROI

The Challenge

A real estate wholesaling company in Tampa, FL was relying on inconsistent referrals and sporadic manual outreach. They had a motivated team to close deals but no reliable way to fill the top of their pipeline with qualified seller leads.

The Solution

Televista deployed a dedicated Growth plan: one full-time trained caller focused exclusively on off-market seller leads in their target zip codes. The caller was trained on the client's ICP, pitch, and objection handling before a single call went out.

Land Investing

Ohio — Consistent Leads for a Land Investor

The Challenge

A land investor in Ohio was new to systematic outbound outreach. They were manually searching for off-market land parcels and relying on inconsistent referrals — spending hours finding deals that still weren't converting reliably.
