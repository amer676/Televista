Our Process

How We Find Off-Market Leads

The best deals never hit the open market. Here's how we surface them before your competition even knows they exist.

01

County Records & Public Data

We pull property data from county assessor records, tax rolls, and public filings to build hyper-targeted lists in your chosen zip codes and counties.

02

Skip Tracing

Every property record gets skip-traced to find current phone numbers. We cross-reference multiple databases to maximize contact rates and reach the actual owner — not a tenant or old number.

03

AI Scoring & List Prioritization

Not all leads are equal. Our AI scoring layer ranks prospects by motivation signals — equity position, days on market, tax delinquency, vacancy indicators — so callers hit the hottest leads first.

04

Targeted Outbound Calling

Trained callers work your lists daily, having real conversations with property owners. When a seller is motivated, they're qualified and the appointment is booked directly on your calendar.

The Difference

Not Just Leads. Qualified Appointments.

Most lead gen companies hand you a spreadsheet and call it a day. We don't stop at "interested." Every lead goes through a double qualification process before it reaches you.

First Pass: Caller Qualification

Your caller confirms motivation, timeline, property condition, price expectations, and decision-making authority during the live conversation. No guessing.

Second Pass: QA Review

Our quality assurance team reviews the call recording to verify the lead meets your criteria before it's marked as qualified. Bad leads get filtered out before they waste your time.

Appointment Booked

Qualified sellers are booked directly on your calendar with full notes — motivation level, property details, asking price, and timeline. You walk in ready to make an offer.
