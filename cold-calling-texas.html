The Texas Problem

Big State, Bigger Competition

Texas is a top destination for real estate investors nationwide. The metros are sprawling, the competition is fierce, and the sellers who need your help most are scattered across hundreds of zip codes. Here's what makes Texas tough to crack alone:

01

Sprawling Metros You Can't Cover Alone

Houston alone has over 180 zip codes. DFW spans four major counties. Manually cold calling your way across these markets is physically impossible for a solo operator or small team. You need dedicated volume to make a dent.

02

DFW & Houston Are Over-Saturated

The biggest Texas metros attract the most investors. Sellers in Dallas and Houston are being contacted by multiple buyers every week. Untrained callers get screened out immediately — you need someone who can break through the noise.

03

Property Tax Liens Create Urgency — If You Move Fast

Texas has some of the highest property tax rates in the nation. Homeowners behind on taxes are motivated but the window is tight — once the lien sale date approaches, they either sell or lose the property. Speed to contact is everything.

FAQ

Texas Cold Calling Questions

How does Televista find off-market leads in Texas?

We assign trained cold callers to your specific Texas counties and zip codes. They work through curated lists — pre-foreclosure, tax delinquent, probate, absentee owner, and vacant property records — and identify homeowners motivated to sell. Qualified leads are handed off to you with full call notes.

Which Texas metros does Televista cover?

We cover all Texas markets. Our busiest campaigns run across Houston (Harris County), DFW (Dallas, Tarrant, Collin, Denton Counties), San Antonio (Bexar County), Austin (Travis County), and Fort Worth. You define the exact zip codes and counties — we dial them.

Can Televista target tax-delinquent properties in Texas?

Absolutely. Texas has some of the highest property tax rates in the country, which creates a steady stream of tax-delinquent and tax-lien-burdened properties. Our callers are trained to engage these homeowners with the right approach — understanding their situation and positioning your offer as a solution.

Is cold calling for real estate legal in Texas?

Yes. Cold calling is legal in Texas when done within TCPA guidelines and the Texas Business & Commerce Code. Televista uses only live human callers, respects the national and state Do Not Call registries, calls within permitted hours, and maintains full compliance documentation for every campaign.
