When to Choose Each

Leads Pro isn't bad. They deliver leads. But "delivering leads" and "building a pipeline machine" are two different things. Here's how to think about it.

When to Choose Televista

You want more than just callers on the phone — you want a system. Televista is for real estate investors, solar teams, roofers, and B2B companies who want the full stack: trained callers, AI lead scoring that actually prioritizes hot leads, CRM integration with whatever you're already using (GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, REsimpli, Podio — we're not picky), n8n/Zapier automation so leads flow where they need to go, and weekly strategy calls where we look at real numbers and adjust. You get a 25-person calling team with 10 on standby, a dedicated account manager, and transparent pricing that won't surprise you. Check our case studies if you want to see what that looks like in practice. We don't just generate leads — we help you close them.

When to Choose Leads Pro

Credit where it's due: Leads Pro's retainer model is straightforward. You pay, you get dedicated callers, data, a dialer, and a guaranteed lead range. If predictable monthly lead volume is your number one priority and you don't need deep CRM customization or AI-driven automation, Leads Pro can work. They're solid for real estate investors who want a simple, reliable calling operation without a lot of moving parts. If your tech stack is basic and you're not looking to build complex automation workflows, their bundled approach keeps things simple. Nothing fancy — but sometimes simple is what you need.

The Bottom Line

Both Televista and Leads Pro will put callers on the phone and generate leads. The difference is what happens around those calls. Televista layers on AI lead scoring, deep CRM integration with every major platform, automation via n8n and Zapier, weekly strategy sessions, and a larger team with built-in redundancy. Leads Pro keeps it simpler — dedicated callers, bundled data and dialing, guaranteed lead ranges. If you want a predictable lead count and nothing else, Leads Pro delivers. If you want a full pipeline operation that scores, routes, automates, and optimizes — Televista is built for that. We're not just a calling company.
