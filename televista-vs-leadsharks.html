Televista vs LeadSharks:
A Partner, or a One-Off Package?

Last updated: June 2026

LeadSharks sells packaged, one-time cold calling campaigns — a set price, a one-time data pull, and a fixed block of caller hours. It's a clean way to run a quick test. But a packaged campaign and a dedicated pipeline partner are not the same thing. We put Televista and LeadSharks side by side — team model, caller hours, data, and what happens after the package runs out. We're the premium, ongoing option here. Here's the honest difference.

When to Choose Each

LeadSharks isn't trying to be your long-term partner — it's selling a packaged campaign. That's a real difference, and it matters depending on what you actually need.

When to Choose Televista

You want outbound that runs every month and gets sharper over time — not a one-and-done burst. Televista is for real estate investors, solar teams, roofers, insurance, and B2B companies who want a full-time dedicated team (25 callers with 10 on standby), AI lead scoring, integration with whatever CRM you already run, n8n and Zapier automation, and weekly strategy calls where we look at the real numbers and adjust. Your pipeline doesn't stop when a package runs out of hours — it keeps compounding. Check our case studies to see what a sustained operation produces versus a single campaign. This is the premium, ongoing option.

When to Choose LeadSharks

Credit where it's due: LeadSharks' one-time packages are a low-commitment way to dip a toe in. You pay once, you get a data pull, a set block of calling hours, and on select packages a 10-leads-in-30-days reload guarantee — no subscription, no ongoing spend. If you just want to test cold calling on a single list without committing to a monthly engagement, that simplicity is genuinely appealing. Just know what it is: roughly 20 caller-hours tied to one campaign, focused on real estate wholesaling. When the hours are used, the calling stops — and so does the momentum.

The Bottom Line

Televista and LeadSharks aren't really the same product. LeadSharks sells a one-time package — a fixed block of caller hours, a single data pull, a guarantee on the tail end, and then it's over until you buy again. Televista is an ongoing partner: a full-time dedicated team of 25, AI lead scoring, automation, weekly strategy calls, a real-time dashboard, and a pipeline that improves the longer it runs. If you want a quick, cheap, one-off test, LeadSharks does that cleanly. If you want a premium operation that builds momentum month after month instead of resetting to zero, that's Televista.
