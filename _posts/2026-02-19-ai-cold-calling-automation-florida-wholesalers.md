## Integrating AI Into Your Workflow

So, how can you create a workflow that effectively integrates AI and human expertise? Here’s a step-by-step approach:

### 1. Set Up Your CRM

Start by ensuring your CRM (like HubSpot) is fully integrated with your calling system. This will allow for seamless data sharing between your calls and your lead management system. With AI, you can automate data entry, reducing errors and freeing your team to focus on closing deals.

### 2. Create AI-Driven Lead Scoring

Use AI algorithms to score your leads based on behaviors such as engagement levels, property interest, and demographic data. This means your callers can prioritize leads that are more likely to convert, making the most of their time.

### 3. Develop Call Scripts

Utilize AI to draft scripts that resonate with your target audience. For example, if you’re targeting sellers facing foreclosure, your script should be empathetic and solution-oriented. Here’s a simple framework for your script:

- **Opening**: “Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Your Company]. I hope you’re doing well today! I’m reaching out because I noticed your property at [Property Address] and wanted to see if you’d consider a cash offer. Is now a good time to chat?”
- **Qualifying**: “Can you share a bit about your experience with the property? Any recent repairs that need attention?”
- **Closing**: “Based on what you’ve shared, I believe we can provide a fair cash offer. Would you be interested in scheduling a time to discuss this further?”

### 4. Implement a Call Cadence

Set a structured call cadence to maximize outreach. Consider using a combination of voicemail drops, follow-ups, and emails to ensure you’re staying top-of-mind for your leads. For instance, the first call could be direct outreach, the second could be a follow-up email summarizing your conversation, and the third might be a voicemail reiterating your offer.

### 5. Track and Analyze KPIs

Monitor key performance indicators such as call connect rates, appointment settings, and conversion rates. This data will guide your adjustments to scripts and calling strategies.
