## Custom Pipeline Architecture: The 5-Strategy Framework for REI Success

**Pipeline 2: Fix & Flip** - Six stages with different timeline expectations, focusing on construction timelines and projected sale prices.

**Pipeline 5: Lead Generation** - Tracks every prospect from "Cold Contact" through "Strategy Determined," automatically moving deals to the appropriate specialized pipeline.

Automation is key. When [CallTools](https://www.calltools.com) logs a call disposition, workflows trigger specific email sequences based on pipeline stage. ## Step-by-Step: Building Your Wholesaling Pipeline with Custom Properties

![Step-by-Step: Building Your Wholesaling Pipeline with Custom Properties](https://source.unsplash.com/800x400/?Step-by-Step%3A%20Building%20Your)


Start with these eight custom properties refined across 40+ wholesaling campaigns. Navigate to Settings > Properties > Contact Properties to create each one:

- **Property Address** (Single-line text) - Critical for automation triggers.
- **ARV** (Number field) - After Repair Value from comps analysis.
- **Repair Estimate** (Number field) - Links to contractor quotes.
- **MAO** (Number field) - Maximum Allowable Offer calculated at 70% ARV minus repairs.
- **Contract Status** (Dropdown) - Options: Unsigned, Pending, Executed, Assigned, Closed.

Integrate [Zillow API via Zapier](https://[zapier](https://zapier.com).com) to pull property data automatically, saving 15 minutes of manual research per lead. The **Deal Stage** progression follows a proven path, with specific automation sequences triggered at each stage.

Most investors overlook **Lead Source** tracking. Create dropdown options for Direct Mail, Cold Calling, PPC, Bandit Signs, and Referrals. This feeds into ROI reporting and identifies productive marketing channels.

Pro tip: Add a **Days on Market** calculated property that updates automatically. Properties sitting 90+ days get different nurture sequences, boosting contract rates by 25% on aged inventory.

## Custom Objects Setup: Properties, Contracts, and Comps Management

Setting up custom objects transforms [HubSpot](https://www.hubspot.com) into a deal analysis powerhouse. Create three core objects:

- **Properties Object:** Central asset database with fields for Property Address, Type, Square Footage, Year Built, and Condition.
- **Contracts Object:** Tracks agreements with fields like Contract Date, Purchase Price, and Status.
- **Comps Object:** Feeds ARV calculations with data on Sale Price, Property Type, and Distance.
